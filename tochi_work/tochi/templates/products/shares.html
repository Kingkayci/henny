SHARES TRADING

TAKE A POSITION ON MORE THAN 9,400 GLOBAL SHARES, INCLUDING 1,000 ETFS. WE OFFER SHARE CFDS ON MANY POPULAR STOCKS, INCLUDING APPLE, BP, LLOYDS BANKING GROUP, TESCO, RIO TINTO AND MANY MORE.

Shares trading are amongst the most popular financial instruments worldwide. CrispFinances offers to its clients a unique capability to trade stocks and futures through reliable platforms. Trading shares through CFDs allows traders to take “short” or “long” positions for a specific stock without the need to own the physical share, similarly to futures or other commodities. If you believe that a share price will fall, you can take a short position (sell) - or take a long position (buy) if you believe the price of the share will rise, and your profits will maximize in line with the position you take and the market movement.

CFD on Shares trading through CrispFinances' multi-award winning services and platforms offer a variety of advantages such as ensuring security through short selling or hedging, especially in volatile markets. Moreover CrispFinances offers access to some of the liquid shares in order to help you maximize your profits.
