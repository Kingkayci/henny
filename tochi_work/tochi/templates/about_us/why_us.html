Why Us

CrisOption is a global online forex broker, which offers some of the tightest spreads in the market for forex trading, through its innovative and user-friendly trading platform, based on the web.

Simplicity In Trading

At CrisOption, our clients are our top priority, and they form the core of our business policies. Therefore, we go out of our way to ensure that Forex trading is a rewarding experience, and more traders are able to take advantage of it. The state-of-the art CrisOption Web Platform was created to ensure that everyone can trade easily and efficiently. You don't need to download software, or read complicated manuals. Connect to the internet, and begin trading right away. Easy as that!


Impeccable Service

The Forex market does not sleep, and neither do we. Our trading platform allows you to participate in Forex trading for as long as the Forex market stays open and alive. Our efficient customer service, as well as our learning center will always be able to provide all the support you need throughout your trading experience.


Investing In Educating Traders

CrisOption has pulled out all the stops in educating traders about the latest market happenings, and providing them with tutorials to teach them the basics of trading. Our daily market reports are comprehensive in nature, and address the major opportunities and issues of the days with regard to Forex trading. Use our videos, information packets, and marketing analysis tools to help you master the art of trading.


Guaranteed Security

CrisOption trading platform meets the highest data security standards on the web. Our security experts have gone to great lengths to provide a secure online virtual trading room. Using a SSL secured server, your personal details are kept in a highly encrypted format. CrispFinances transforms the Forex market into a safe and secure trading environment, allowing traders to trade with confidence.


Other Key Features

  • Back-to-back execution with some of the world's premier banks/financial institutions, all competing to provide CrispFinances the best bid/asking prices.
  • No dealer intervention on trades.
  • No conflict of interest between the broker and trader.
  • Interactive Courses for learning the tricks of Forex Trading.
